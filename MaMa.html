Maisa Zoghier

Maisa Zoghier, a multifaceted artist born in Syria in 1988 and currently residing in the United Arab Emirates, embodies a captivating fusion of heritage and creative expression. With a diverse range of artistic talents, Maisa seamlessly navigates various mediums, leaving her indelible mark on the world of art. Whether it be the rich cultural tapestry of her Syrian roots influencing her brushstrokes, or the contemporary influences of her UAE home shaping her digital works, Maisa's art reflects a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation. Her creations, spanning painting, sculpture, and digital art, tell stories of identity, resilience, and the intricate interplay of cultures. In the vibrant artistic landscape of the UAE, Maisa Zoghier's contributions stand out, resonating with the dynamic spirit of the region. Her journey as an artist mirrors the unfolding narratives of the places she has called home, capturing the essence of both the personal and the universal in her evocative creations.
